Dear TOPMed Investigators,
 
The NIH Common Fund (CF) is requesting research applications (RFA-RM-26-017) encouraging the use of Common Fund data. To be eligible, applicants must propose the use of two or more NIH Common Fund (CF) program data sets (a full list of eligible Common Fund data sets is available below). Applicants may also propose the use of additional data sets, including other NIH Common Fund data sets not listed, other NIH data sets, and publicly available non-NIH data sets.
 
Full Text of the Funding Opportunity Announcement: Pilot Projects Enhancing Utility and Usage of Common Fund Data Sets (RFA-RM-26-017).
 
Applications are due June 23, 2026, by 5:00 PM local time of applicant organization. More information can be found on the Common Fund Data Ecosystem website. All questions are welcome, contact the CFDE Program Team (CFDE@od.nih.gov) or George Papanicolaou (george.papanicolaou@nih.gov) for additional information about this opportunity. Please note that Early Stage Investigators (ESI) are  encouraged to apply, as receipt of an R03 award does not impact ESI status.
